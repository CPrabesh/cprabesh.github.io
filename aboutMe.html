About the Author

Hello! I am Prabesh Chhetri, a student currently based in the Lilburn and Snellville area of Georgia. I built this website as a portfolio project for my IT 3203 course. Alongside my IT studies, my academic coursework has also included Macroeconomics, Accounting, Calculus, and Psychology, which helps me maintain a well-rounded perspective on business and technology.

Outside of my own coursework, I work as a programming tutor, helping students master Python. I've also been actively developing my professional skill set, recently preparing for roles like a Customer Experience Associate at PNC Bank. When I'm not studying or coding, you can usually find me streaming music on Spotify, researching automotive projects, or baking—my absolute favorite is Tres Leches cake with a classic whipped cream frosting.

This website is a class project for the Kennesaw State University IT 3203 course.
